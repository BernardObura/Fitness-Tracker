Full Body Strength

Intermediate
8 weeks Goal: Strength 4 days/week
About This Plan

A comprehensive workout plan designed to build strength across all major muscle groups through progressive overload. This intermediate-level program focuses on compound exercises and gradual progression to help you build strength efficiently. The plan is structured as a 4-day split, targeting different muscle groups each day while allowing adequate recovery time.

The workouts are designed to be completed in 45-60 minutes, making it accessible for those with busy schedules. Each exercise includes recommended sets, reps, and rest periods to optimize your training.

Day 1: Upper Body Focus

Chest, shoulders, and triceps

Barbell Bench Press Chest
4 sets × 8-10 reps • Rest: 90s
Overhead Press Shoulders
3 sets × 8-10 reps • Rest: 90s
Incline Dumbbell Press Upper Chest
3 sets × 10-12 reps • Rest: 60s
Lateral Raises Shoulders
3 sets × 12-15 reps • Rest: 60s
Tricep Pushdowns Triceps
3 sets × 12-15 reps • Rest: 60s
Day 2: Lower Body Focus

Quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves

Barbell Squat Quadriceps
4 sets × 8-10 reps • Rest: 120s
Romanian Deadlift Hamstrings
3 sets × 10-12 reps • Rest: 90s
Leg Press Quadriceps
3 sets × 10-12 reps • Rest: 90s
Walking Lunges Quadriceps/Glutes
3 sets × 12 steps per leg • Rest: 60s
Standing Calf Raises Calves
4 sets × 15-20 reps • Rest: 45s
Day 3: Rest Day

Active recovery or light cardio

Take this day to rest and recover. You may perform light activities such as walking, swimming, or stretching to promote blood flow and recovery.

Day 4: Pull Day

Back and biceps

Pull-Ups / Lat Pulldowns Back
4 sets × 8-10 reps • Rest: 90s
Bent-Over Barbell Rows Back
3 sets × 8-10 reps • Rest: 90s
Seated Cable Rows Middle Back
3 sets × 10-12 reps • Rest: 60s
Barbell Bicep Curls Biceps
3 sets × 10-12 reps • Rest: 60s
Hammer Curls Biceps/Forearms
3 sets × 12-15 reps • Rest: 60s
